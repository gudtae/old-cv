ABOUT

I am a PhD candidate in Bayesian Machine Learning in EURECOM, Sophia-Antipolis, France. I will get the title in early 2023. Currently, I am specializing in Bayesian Methods for Machine Learning, Kernel Methods and Gaussian Processes with application to optical computing hardware. In the past, I worked on application of Machine Learning to Neuroscience in general and Brain-Computer Interfaces in particular. I was foucused on adopting advances of Deep Learning method for classification of Electroencephalographic signals including development of Neural Network architectures, Transfer learning and Meta-learning techniques.
